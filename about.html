Hi, I'm Loes

In 2006, even before we had computer with internet at our home, I discovered how to style webpages using CSS and not long after that I was building websites with HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL and Adobe Photoshop for everyone who needed one. I loved every aspect of it, from designing logos to scripting in PHP.

During my studies (Computer Science) I spend uncountably many hours programming in Python and I learned many other cool things like machine learning, (genomic) data science, linguistics and statistics.

After my graduation I was working as a (Python) research support programmer, but as a result of the corona lockdown my projects gradually switched from Python to JavaScript in order to support web-based research and soon I rediscovered my true passion. For me, digital development and design is like magic: starting with absolutely nothing you can create the most beautiful and awesome things.

Currently I design and develop for the web on a daily basis, which includes UX and graphic design, developing the front-ends for websites, dashboards and API's, building small WordPress websites and search engine optimization.
